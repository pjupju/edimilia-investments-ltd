Marketing a Table Tennis Business 101 – Everything You Need to Know

reading time: 10 minutes

When you think of spectator sports, table tennis hardly features among the top five events that come to mind. Most people associate the sport with their grandparents’ basement, middle school gym class, and in extreme cases – Forrest Gump. Yet, it is so much more than that. It is a highly-competitive Olympic sport that requires a serious amount of athleticism and skill to master. Despite this fact, it does not enjoy the massive worldwide popularity that other sports have. That is precisely why it is important for anyone marketing a table tennis business to get it right from the get-go. You need to have the right set of information to stay ahead of the competition and target the people that matter – table tennis lovers. But, make no mistake about it. While it may not have a huge fanatic following like that witnessed in other sports like soccer, it still has a sizable and loyal fan-base that you can tap into and generate substantial revenue from it.
